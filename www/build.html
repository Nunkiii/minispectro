A Google search for "DVD spectrograph" will give you many links to projects from which you can get inspiration to build your own DVD based spectrograph. Here we present a simple and easy to build model, initially designed by Arvind Paranjpye (Inter-University Centre for Astronomy & Astrophysics - India) http://www.iucaa.ernet.in/~scipop/.

  • Reflexion spectrograph: This nice spectrograph paper model is to be placed on top of a CD or DVD. A webcam or smartphone can be attached at the eye location to make a suitable instrument to use with WebSpectro.
